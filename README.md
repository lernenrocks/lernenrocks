# Hi, I'm Andy 👋

People and technology – that's been my constant for as long as I can remember.

## The backstory

I spent nine years working in youth welfare, including 24-hour shifts with teenagers and young adults. In a field full of social workers, I happened to be one of the few who could sit down and actually help with the math homework. That says a lot about how I ended up where I am.

## What I do

By day I work as an **Account Manager** at a software company – onboarding new customers and making sure they actually get what they signed up for. On the side, as a **self-employed tutor**, I teach math – from grade 7 all the way up to introductory university modules for non-math degrees – and occasionally OOP.

Somewhere in between I studied Cognitive Informatics in Bielefeld on the side – which, unsurprisingly, is also one of the reasons I successfully dropped out.

## Why I'm here

I'm neither a passionate developer nor a tech expert – I just refuse to write sloppy code for something I actually care about.

My GitHub is mostly a fireproof backup for **TerraControl** – a terrarium control system built on an ESP32, because why not. Sensor data, smart plugs, SD and database logging, and maybe a UI if the hardware cooperates.

*A work in progress. Like most things worth building.*
